What is Pathjump?

Pathjump is the young professional network. Pathjump lets you explore careers, discover what jobs you are best suited for based on your profile, apply to amazing internships and jobs, and provides guidance to improve your chances of getting those jobs. Pathjump helps you find your dream path!
